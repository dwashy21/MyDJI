1

Turn on the OSMO

Turn on the Osmo. Do not touch the gimbal. Hold the handle still when the Osmo is starting up.

2

Connect to the Wi-Fi network

On your mobile device, connect to the ‘OSMO_XXXXXX’ Wi-Fi network and enter the default password（12341234）. Then launch the DJI GO app.

3

Activate OSMO

Follow the instructions to activate the Osmo if you are using it for the first time. This requires an internet connection.

4

Enter Camera View

Enter Camera View. You will see what the camera captures in real time if it is connected successfully.
